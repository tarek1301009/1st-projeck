We are the first facility in Southeast Florida to have performed Laser Cataract and Astigmatism Surgery with a 3-Distance Multifocal Implant. Basic cataract surgery is performed using a small blade to make an incision and remove the cataract without correcting the astigmatism (the oval shape of the cornea in front of the eye)

During Laser Cataract and Astigmatism Surgery, the femtolaser allows us to correct the astigmatism, make all the incisions, and divide the cataract into over 50 small pieces so it can be easily aspirated.
